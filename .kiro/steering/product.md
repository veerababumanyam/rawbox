# Product Overview

RawBox is a **SaaS (Software as a Service)** professional photography client gallery platform that enables photographers to manage, share, and deliver photos to clients. The application serves as a comprehensive multi-tenant studio management system with AI-powered features.

## SaaS Architecture

- **Multi-tenant**: Each photographer has their own isolated workspace
- **Subscription-based**: Tiered pricing with feature access controls
- **Cloud-hosted**: Scalable infrastructure supporting 20,000+ photographer customers
- **Self-service**: Photographers can sign up, configure, and manage their own accounts

## Core Features

- **Gallery Management**: Create and organize photo galleries with sub-galleries, support for both photos and videos
- **Client Portal**: Dedicated client views with password protection, email registration, and customizable branding
- **Print Album Designer**: Visual design tool for creating print-ready photo albums with spreads, layouts, and proofing workflows
- **AI Integration**: Powered by Google Gemini for photo analysis, face detection, smart curation, and content generation
- **People & Face Tagging**: Automatic face detection and manual tagging system for organizing photos by person
- **Client Management**: CRM-style client database with contact information, social profiles, and project history
- **Photographer Profile**: Public-facing profile pages with company and personal information visibility controls

## User Roles

- **Photographer/Admin**: Full access to all features, gallery management, client management, and settings
- **Client**: View-only access to assigned galleries with optional download, favoriting, and photo selection capabilities

## Key Workflows

1. Create gallery → Upload photos → Organize into sub-galleries → Share with client
2. AI-powered photo analysis and tagging for efficient organization
3. Design print albums with drag-and-drop interface and client proofing
4. Manage client relationships and track project history
